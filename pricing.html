Questions

Why choose Wizerflow over an SDR?

Get 17x more output at 30% of the cost of an in-house SDR ($8,595/mo). No benefits or severance costs, just predictable results.

5 meetings/month sounds specific. What if we need more?

5 is our baseline guarantee for the standard program, ensuring you a consistent flow while fine tuning our AI outreach system to your niche. We typically deliver more and will customize your package for higher volume once we establish the successful calibration.

How much more cost-effective is this, really?

When you take into account payroll, benefits, severance, and the opportunity cost of hiring, our service is ~30% of the price of hiring in-house, and ~70% of the price of hiring an agency. You also get access to two world-class ops executives that have helped scale several 8-figure companies, as well as a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

How does payment work?

Our pricing is mostly based on results. After our FREE consultation and agreement on the project scope, investment and guarantee, you'll be taken to a Stripe Checkout page. After you pay, we'll automatically create a subscription for you that renews on the same day every month. You can cancel or pause at any time.

How quickly do we see results?

First campaign results varies by plan. With our Standard plan, clients typically begin seeing meaningful results within 21-28 days as we optimize targeting and messaging. Pro plan clients benefit from accelerated implementation and dedicated resources, often achieving significant results in as little as 10 days. Both plans include our proven methodology and continuous optimization to ensure sustainable growth.

What's your refund policy?

If you're not 100% satisfied with your service, we'll keep working for free until you are. We are committed to ensuring your satisfaction and will work diligently to address any concerns during your notice period.

"Guaranteed meetings" sounds too good to be true. What’s the catch? How do you define a ‘qualified’ meeting for payment?

No catch — just a clear standard. You only pay when a prospect not only books a call but shows up and meets strict qualification criteria. Here's what qualifies:

  • ✅ Booked: The decision-maker booked the call.
  • ✅ Attended: The decision-maker attend the meeting. (unless your team don't show up).
  • ✅ Relevant: They previously openly discuss pain points your solution addresses.

No-shows? Unqualified prospects? You're not charged — and if prepaid, we refund that meeting. You pay only for real, attended opportunities.

How do you integrate with our existing CRM and sales process?

We aim for seamless integration. Typically:

  • Booking: We can book directly into your sales reps' calendars via tools like Calendly or Chili Piper.
  • CRM Entry: Depending on your preference and CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.), we can either manually provide meeting details for your team to log or explore lightweight integration options (like BCC'ing emails or using Zapier) to push basic contact/meeting info.

Our goal is to feed qualified meetings into your existing funnel with minimal friction.

We onboard you within 24 hours, tailor outreach strategies to your needs, and start booking ideal clients ASAP
